Who

madAI is the machine learning club at James Madison High School in Vienna, Virginia. We are entirely student-run, and our sponsor is the JMHS computer science teacher: Ms. Listman.

  • Our Team

What

We are dedicated to educating the next generation in topics of AI and machine learning. Besides creating our own curriculum to educate our students in an easy, accessible fasion, we also work towards hosting tech events and speaker panels.

  • The Curriculum

Why

Due to Madison's lack of high-level technology education, madAI takes initiative to educate our students on the topics of AI and machine learning. Our founders were passionate about these areas, and wanted to bring a deeper level of education to the students of Madison.
